Syndication Service

Available Feeds

Syndication service creates the rss/atom feeds based on the search query provided, use the following url to get the feed.

  • Atom feed - ?SearchQuery or /Atom?SearchQuery
  • Atom feed with page size - /PageSize?SearchQuery or /Atom/PageSize?SearchQuery
  • Rss Feed - /Rss?SearchQuery
  • Rss feed with page size - /Rss/PageSize?SearchQuery
  • Help - /Help
